Introduction

This policy is intended to clearly explain how the programs distributed through DomePrograms process user data. Each author is responsible for the information provided in the "Content Privacy" section of their program, but this page provides a general overview to help users understand what data may be collected, how it is used, and what rights are guaranteed. Specifically, this policy clarifies:

  • Who processes the data: the data controller is the author of the program indicated on the relevant page.
  • What data may be collected: personal information, preferences, technical data, file access, internet connections, and anonymous data for program improvement.
  • For what purposes: software operation, customization, security, improvement of services and other purposes declared by the author.
  • Legal basis for processing: Data processing is based on the user's consent, use of the program, or other legitimate bases declared by the author.
  • Data Sharing: Authors can declare whether data is shared with first parties (only the author) or third parties (other companies or developers).
  • Retention periods: the data is retained for the time necessary for the purposes declared by the program author.
  • Security Measures: DomePrograms and the authors take measures to protect data, as outlined in the dedicated section.
  • User rights: The user can request access, rectification, erasure, limitation of processing, data portability, and opposition, as provided by applicable law.
  • Data transfers: If data is transferred outside the European Union, the data controller must indicate the safeguards adopted.

This introduction provides an overview of data processing practices. Specific information may vary from program to program and is provided directly by the author in the "Content Privacy" section.
